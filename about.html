Welcome to the Jordan Consulate in Nepal

About the Consulate

Your Gateway to Jordan in Nepal

Our Mission

The Office of the Honorary Consul of The Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan in Nepal serves as the primary diplomatic representation of Jordan in Nepal. We are dedicated to strengthening bilateral relations and providing comprehensive consular services.

Core Objectives:

  • Facilitate diplomatic relations between Jordan and Nepal
  • Provide efficient consular services
  • Promote cultural understanding
  • Support business and trade initiatives

Our Services

  • Visa processing and information
  • Document authentication and legalization
  • Consular assistance for Jordanian citizens
  • Trade and investment facilitation
  • Cultural exchange program coordination
  • Educational cooperation support

Contact Information

Diplomatic Cooperation

Nepal and Jordan have maintained strong diplomatic ties since establishing relations. Both nations work together in various international forums and support each other on matters of mutual interest.

Key Areas of Cooperation:

  • Regular high-level diplomatic visits
  • Mutual support in international forums
  • Cooperation in regional peace initiatives
  • Joint efforts in global challenges

Economic Relations

The economic partnership between Nepal and Jordan continues to grow, encompassing various sectors:

  • Bilateral trade agreements
  • Investment opportunities
  • Tourism promotion
  • Technical cooperation
  • Labor agreements

Cultural Exchange

Cultural ties between Nepal and Jordan are strengthened through various initiatives:

  • Educational scholarships
  • Cultural festivals and exhibitions
  • Academic cooperation
  • Art and cultural programs

About Jordan

Discover the Hashemite Kingdom

Country Overview

Quick Facts:

  • Capital: Amman
  • Population: 10.2 million (2021)
  • Official Language: Arabic
  • Currency: Jordanian Dinar (JOD)
  • Government: Constitutional Monarchy

Culture & Heritage

Jordan boasts a rich cultural heritage spanning thousands of years:

  • Ancient archaeological sites including Petra
  • Rich Islamic and Christian history
  • Traditional Bedouin culture
  • Modern arts and culture scene
  • Renowned hospitality

Modern Jordan

Today's Jordan is a blend of tradition and modernity:

  • Stable and progressive economy
  • Leading education hub in the Middle East
  • Advanced healthcare system
  • Growing technology sector
  • Popular tourist destination
