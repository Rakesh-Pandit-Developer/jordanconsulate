Our Services

Consular Services

Our consulate provides a wide range of consular services, including passport renewals, notarial services, legal documentation, and emergency assistance for Jordanian citizens.

Visa Information

We offer complete guidance on visa categories, application procedures, and required documentation to facilitate travel to Jordan for tourism, business, or education.

Information Services

Explore reliable information about Jordan, including its culture, history, tourism attractions, business opportunities, and important travel updates for visitors and residents.

Education and Scholarship Guidance

We assist students in exploring educational opportunities in Jordan, providing information on universities, scholarships, and admission processes to support their academic journey.
