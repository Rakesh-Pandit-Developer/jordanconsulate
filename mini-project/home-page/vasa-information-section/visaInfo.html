Visa Information for the Kingdom of Jordan

In order to enter the Kingdom of Jordan, the applicant must be a holder of a valid visa. A visa is the permit to enter, stay in, and leave the territory of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan, as prescribed by the visa category classification. The terms and conditions should be followed as prescribed by Jordanian law and the relevant international guidelines.

To be granted a visa, the applicant must meet all the necessary requirements. However, the issuance of the visa is not guaranteed and is at the sole discretion of the Embassy or Consulate of Jordan.

The Honorary Consulate of Jordan in Kathmandu does not handle the submission of application documents, their processing, or the issuance of visas. All visa applications must be submitted directly to the Embassy or Consulate of Jordan for processing.

Visa Requirements: It is necessary to submit a completed visa application form along with all required documents. These documents should be submitted in person to the Embassy of Jordan for further processing.

For more information about the visa categories, requirements, and application process, please refer to the official Visa Guidelines.
